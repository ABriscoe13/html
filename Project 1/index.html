The 1989 LTD Crown Victoria

The 1989 LTD Crown Victoria is one of my favorite sedans from the 80s.
The Ford 5.0L V8 is a powerful and reliable engine that could survive an apocalypse. On top of that, this thing is basically a four-door Foxbody.

Specs

  • Engine: 5.0L V8
  • Horsepower: 150 hp
  • Torque: 275 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 10-12 seconds
  • Top Speed: 120 mph

Why I love it

  1. It has that classic 80s boxy design
  2. Super torquey engine
  3. Spacious interior, it's like driving a couch
  4. It just exudes that American look I love in these old sedans
